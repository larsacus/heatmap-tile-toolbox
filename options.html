Setup

  1. Login to strava.com with your account.
  2. Visit each heatmap tiles auth endpoint to associate your login cookies from strava.com with each instance:
    • https://heatmap-external-a.strava.com/auth
    • https://heatmap-external-b.strava.com/auth
    • https://heatmap-external-c.strava.com/auth
Upon success, it should say: 
Logged in as AthleteId(XXXXXXX), cloudfront cookies set

Usage

  1. Strava iD: You can now use the high-resolution map data from Strava in Strava iD.
  2. Trailforks: As a bonus, you can also now see the heatmap tiles in Trailforks when the "ridelines" map tiles are enabled. Even in the path editor!
